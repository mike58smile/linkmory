LinkMory

To revive the memories forgotten...

LinkMory offers quick access to hard-to-find digital content such as photos, videos, websites using special NFC stickers and cards.

How it works

1
Buy LinkMory tag and download
our simple LinkMory app
 (on Play store, App store)
2
Link your LinkMory tag in app to
any website – it takes just
couple of seconds! 
3
Anybody can now scan the LinkMory tag without the app and it quickly redirects him to the linked content
